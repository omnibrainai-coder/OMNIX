OMNIX PRIVACY POLICY

Last Modified: July 2, 2026

1. Data Minimization and Zero Background Tracking

Omnix enforces strict data boundary protocols. Hardware mechanisms, specifically the device camera arrays, microphone feeds, and location telemetry sensors, operate exclusively within a foreground active execution context. When the application context shifts to the background layer, all hardware listener loops are structurally terminated within a 99.5% operational matrix to prevent any unauthorized background data harvesting.

2. Data Sovereignty and End-to-End Encryption

User message payloads, private communication nodes, and local data interactions managed via the database pipeline are mathematically obfuscated utilizing strict client-side encryption layers. No administrative agent, host provider, or primary application developer maintains a decryption protocol or structural bypass mechanism to access plaintext communication architectures. We strictly enforce a zero-third-party data selling policy.

3. Account Invalidation and Comprehensive Data Deletion

ACCOUNT DELETION TIMELINE: USERS RETAIN THE UNCONDITIONAL RIGHT TO INITIATE ACCOUNT TERMINATION VIA THE APP SETTINGS. THE ERASURE PROTOCOL TAKES UP TO 24 HOURS TO COMPLETELY PURGE FROM THE PRODUCTION DIRECTORIES.

Upon triggering the data erasure sequence, all authentication tokens hosted via the Supabase cluster, relational metadata records, encrypted chat arrays, and individual profile schemas enter a deletion queue. Within a maximum of 24 hours, all records are permanently deleted, and a final automated confirmation email will be dispatched to the user's registered address certifying the successful removal of all personal assets.

4. Data Protection Officer and Grievance Desk

For any privacy-related questions, comprehensive data access requests, or specific grievances regarding your user information ownership, users can contact our dedicated Data Protection desk directly. All compliance inquiries are addressed strictly under structured evaluation metrics at our official support gateway: support@omnix.shadow.

5. Policy Updates and Mandatory Notification Manifest

Omnix reserves the right to modify or completely update this Privacy Policy framework to reflect regulatory shifts or architectural patches. Users will be formally notified of any material changes via explicit in-app banners or notification modules before those structural adjustments become officially effective on the network layer.
